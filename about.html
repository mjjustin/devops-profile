🎯 Why We Exist

The Talent Crisis We Set Out to Solve

The world was facing a fundamental mismatch: businesses needed skilled workers faster than the talent market could provide. A typical hire takes 42 days and costs $4,700 in recruitment alone — before the first day of work. Small companies couldn't compete with BigTech salaries. Enterprises drowned in procurement bureaucracy.

We asked a simple question: "What if 80% of knowledge work could be performed by AI agents that deploy in hours, not months?" That question became Orange Bytes.
